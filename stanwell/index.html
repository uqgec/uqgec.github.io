Stanwell

Online monitoring desiccation behaviour of Stanwell coal tailings in the column located on the roof of Building 50 at UQ

Description

The experiment aims to investigate the desiccation behaviour of coal tailings obtained fron Stanwell

Parameters monitored includes
-> 10 soil moisture sensors
-> 9 soil suction sensors
-> 7 soil salinity sensors
-> 2 Pressure transducers

The historical snapshot can be found from Stanwell
